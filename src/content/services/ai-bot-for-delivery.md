## The Most Expensive Question in Logistics

"Where is my package?" — four words that cost delivery companies millions in support overhead every year. Industry data suggests that tracking inquiries account for up to 70% of all customer service contacts in last-mile delivery. Each of those contacts costs $5-12 to handle through a live agent. Multiply that across thousands of daily deliveries and the numbers become staggering.

An AI delivery bot eliminates the vast majority of these contacts by giving customers instant, self-service access to the information they actually want.

## What the Bot Handles — So Your Team Doesn't Have To

The delivery bot sits between your logistics infrastructure and your customers, translating complex shipment data into simple, conversational answers:

- **Order tracking** — Customer provides an order number or phone number and gets real-time status: picked up, in transit, out for delivery, delivered, with an ETA window.
- **Delivery rescheduling** — If no one is home, the bot offers reschedule options, redirect to a pickup point, or safe-place instructions.
- **Delay management** — Weather disruptions, customs holds, or route changes trigger proactive notifications so customers hear about delays before they experience them.
- **Returns initiation** — Wrong item or damaged goods? The bot captures photos, generates return labels, and schedules pickup — all within the chat.
- **Proof of delivery** — Customers request delivery confirmation photos and signatures directly through the bot.

## Why Traditional Support Cannot Keep Up

Delivery operations have a unique problem: demand for support spikes unpredictably. A snowstorm in one region, a carrier delay in another, a viral product launch — suddenly your support queue has 500 people waiting. Hiring seasonal agents is expensive and slow. Training them takes weeks.

The AI bot absorbs these spikes without blinking. It handles 10 inquiries or 10,000 with the same sub-10-second response time. During peak holiday seasons, when competitor support lines show 45-minute hold times, your customers get answers before they finish typing.

## Measurable Impact on Your Bottom Line

Companies using our delivery bot report a 70% reduction in support ticket volume within the first 60 days. Customer satisfaction scores climb to 90% because people get instant answers instead of waiting. And your human agents?
